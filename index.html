About the College


SSN School of Management is a new generation B-School established with the objective of nurturing a powerful resource base of Managerial talent and Computer Application specialists. The Institution has been founded by Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman- HCL Technologies Ltd., a pioneer in the field of Information Technology in India. It was born out of Dr.Shiv Nadar’s ardent desire to give back something significant to the society that nurtured him. It only follows that the Institution reflects the ideals of its founder and seeks to achieve excellence in education.

Located in a 250-acre idyllic campus on the Old Mahabalipuram Road, the IT Corridor of Chennai, SSN SoM is a constituent of the SSN College of Engineering (SSNCE), affiliated to the Anna University, Chennai, and has the approval of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.

Within a short span of time, the Institution has made rapid strides towards establishing its programs, with wide acceptance  from industry. As an Institution committed to excellence in education, our admission policy is strongly in favor of merit.
